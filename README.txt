The Game:
The Game is a Tower Defense Game, where people have become AI-addicts and are causing chaos.
Using Towers, the player places defenses such as "Printer", "Judge", and "HR Building" in order to get people under control.
It teaches people about the dangers of overreliance on LLM's. And some of the things that might be done against different kinds of misuse of AI.
